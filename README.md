# GBCLXML
A variation of GBCL that parses a custom XML language.

## Concept
Probabilistic Models
  * a probabilistic parser is able to handle selecting between three possible prolog like variables, or alternative XML formatting with specific tags.
  * The parse also has auto correction characteristics that allows it to repair badly formed prolog or xml rules.
  * Other probabilistic models predict things like a French version of the 5Ws, such as who, what, when, where, why, and how. Along with Jungian mythic structure.
  * Some models are designed specifically to predict monster, trap, loot, mutations, and linguistic drift over time.
  * Specific models combine live modeling and pre-trained models, such as the three currency economic simulation.

Hopscotch And Zombie UI Mechanics
  * Hopscotch zombie is a variation of hopscotch where if you you gradually lose legs or arms.
  * This is mixed with desert survival mechanics where you crashed your spaceship in a desert with overgrown poison cactus.
  * Your guided by a case officer that advises you about your current health status, and what your sanity and trust status is and gathers information on a Maids activities.
  * A maid will occassionally try to intercept your activities inside of a haunted mansion.

Parallel Cartography
  * Parallel cartography allows you to observe three different shortest path search algorithms.
  * Each class is a subclass of the main machine learning class, and therefore inherits everything from that class plus its own functionality.
